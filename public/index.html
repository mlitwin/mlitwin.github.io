Preliminaries to a Software Testing Manifesto

I’m not quite ready for a manifesto. Still gathering my own thoughts, and trying to understand software testing terminology and methodologies. But - feels like there is a fair amount of confusion about how it’s supposed to work even in the best case. At a high level, I’ll try to break down what software tests are, why we use them, and try to elucidate the terminology used in describing the kinds and properties of various forms of testing.
